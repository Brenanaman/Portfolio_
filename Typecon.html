Typecon

Program » Adobe InDesign

Format » 11in x 17in

For this project, we were supposed to translate the verbal structure of a message into visual form. Our objective was to explore various ways to organize information and impart an order of importance to the message. From the body text I received, I created 6 plates to show different ways to provide hierarchy within different grid systems. I then went in and created a poster that showed everything I learned while using the Bauhaus style.
